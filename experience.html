EXPERIENCE

VIDHI BAGADIA



  1. SMART INDIA HACKATHON, 2017


    SMART INDIA HACKATHON 2017 was the first ever debut hackathon organised by the Government of India to spread and receive ideas on various issues concerning the different departments of ministries.

    Out of thousands of teams, my team, DIGI8, grabbed the chance to enter into the finale for one of the Ministries.

    The Project was based on making a centralised portal for NE tourism. The team was one of the top eight teams that received the chance to formally present their idea to a group of experienced jury.

    Project Name: SCENES



  2. IDEATHON 2017: YOUTH INNOVATIONS FOR A BRIGHTER ASSAM


    IDEATHON 2017 was an Idea + Implementation Hackathon organised by a module of Goverment of Assam, viz., The Centre for Sustainable Development Goals and UNDP.

    The teams had to present either a social or technical solution to the problem statements. We had opted for a mixture of both. Among teams from all over Assam, our team had secured one of the two winning spots whose ideas are to be implemented in a small village.

    Project Name: Sarathi
